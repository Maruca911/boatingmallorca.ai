Regatta Season in Mallorca: Events Calendar & Spectating

Published January 2026 | Category: Events

Mallorca hosts multiple regattas throughout the year, attracting sailors from across Europe. Whether you want to participate or spectate, Mallorca's competitive sailing scene offers unforgettable experiences.

Major Spring Regattas (March-May)

Copa del Rey (King's Cup) - August

Europe's largest sailing regatta with 100+ boats. World-class competition, international media coverage, prestigious venue. Palma waterfront vibrates with activity.

Best for: Spectating | Fleet: 50-100+ boats | Class: ORC, Maxi, TP52

Summer Regattas (June-September)

Trofeo Princesa Sofía (Princess Sofia Trophy) - April

Olympic-class training ground. Attracts future Olympians and established competitors. Intense racing across multiple classes and youth divisions.

Best for: Olympic enthusiasts | Fleet: 200+ boats

Mallorca Superyacht Cup - June

Luxury superyacht racing with spectacular vessels 30m+. Close quarters, technical racing in Palma Bay. Glamorous event with international guests.

Best for: Superyacht fans | Fleet: 15-30 superyachts

Participation Guide

  • Entry requirements: Valid racing qualifications (usually RYA or equivalent), insured boat
  • Registration: 4-12 weeks prior to event
  • Entry fees: 500-2,000 EUR typically, depends on boat class
  • Boat classes: ORC (rating-based), IRC (high-performance), TP52 (one-design), Maxi (large boats)
  • Youth categories: Many regattas include junior/youth divisions

Spectating Tips

  • Best viewpoint: Paseo Marítimo in Palma for waterfront views
  • On-water viewing: Charter a spectator boat (300-500 EUR)
  • Race schedules: Typically 10 AM-5 PM daily
  • Bring provisions: Sun protection, water, snacks
  • Photography: Binoculars recommended for identifying boats

How to Get Involved

As crew: Join boat owner's team as volunteer crew. Exciting racing experience without boat ownership.

As boat owner: Charter a competitive boat for the regatta period.

As amateur sailor: Join amateur racing divisions in smaller boats or catamarans.
